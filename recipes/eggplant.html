Eggplant

Description

Definitions of eggplant. noun. egg-shaped vegetable having a shiny skin typically dark purple but occasionally white or yellow. synonyms: aubergine, mad apple. solanaceous vegetable.


Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • breadcrumbs
  • tomatoes
  • arious herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Slice eggplant into 1cm-thick rounds
  2. Spray both sides lightly with oilolive oil cooking spray
  3. arrange in a single layer on oven trays
  4. Roast for 20 minutes
  5. turning after 10 minutes
  6. or until tender and golden
  7. Set aside to cool.
